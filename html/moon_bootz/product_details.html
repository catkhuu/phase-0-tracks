Step Back into 1969

Moon Bootz came during an era of scientific exploration, when humankind was fascinated by space travel to the moon. All of this was achieved on July 16, 1969 during the Apollo 11 lunar landing when astronaut Edwin "Buzz" Aldrin remarked, "...one small step for a man, one giant leap for mankind." Moon Bootz hark back to an era of innovation and exploration while looking cool, tall, and out of this world. Step back into 1969 with us, and be cool.

Product Features

  • Synthetic upper
  • Rubber sole
  • Insulated with nylon for warmth because temperatures in space drop to below freezing
  • Slip-on boots
  • Slipper-like feel with non-designated right and left boots. Each boot looks the same.
  • Light materials mean a lighter you in space and on Earth.
  • Heel height: 1 1/2"
  • Platform: 3/4"
